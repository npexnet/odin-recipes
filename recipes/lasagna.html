lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and often meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • Ground Beef: 1 pound (450 grams)
  • Ground Pork: 1/2 pound (225 grams)
  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 2-3 cloves, minced
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes: 2 cans (28 ounces each)
  • Tomato Paste: 1 can (6 ounces)
  • Tomato Sauce: 1 can (15 ounces)
  • Water: 1/2 cup (120 ml)
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Dried Basil Leaves: 2 tablespoons
  • Fennel Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Italian Seasoning: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 tablespoon
  • Ground Black Pepper: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Fresh Parsley: 1/4 cup, chopped

For the Cheese Mixture

  • Ricotta Cheese: 15 ounces (425 grams)
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese: 3/4 cup
  • Fresh Parsley: 2 tablespoons, chopped

For Assembling

  • Lasagna Noodles: 12-15 noodles (uncooked or pre-cooked, depending on the type)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 16 ounces (shredded)

Steps

  • Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    1. In a large pot, cook the ground beef, ground pork, and chopped onions over medium heat until browned. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
    2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add sugar (if using), basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  • Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    1. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg, grated Parmesan, and chopped parsley.
  • Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions if using traditional noodles. If using no-boil noodles, ensure the sauce has enough moisture to cook them during baking.
  • Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce.
    3. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with a third of the mozzarella cheese.
    4. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, then add another layer of noodles.
    5. Repeat the layers (cheese mixture, mozzarella, meat sauce, noodles) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and remaining mozzarella on top.
  • Bake:
    1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray).
    2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
    3. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving.
